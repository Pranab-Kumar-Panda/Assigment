FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your learning journey.

Do I need any prior coding experience?+
Absolutely not! Our courses are designed for complete beginners as well as experienced developers looking to level up. We start from absolute basics and gradually move to advanced concepts at a comfortable pace. The only requirement is a computer and willingness to learn.
Are the certificates industry-recognized?+
Yes! Our certificates are recognized by 200+ hiring partners including startups and MNCs across India. Each certificate includes a unique verification link you can share on LinkedIn, your resume, or with employers. Several of our hiring partners actively look for BlueWave-certified candidates.
How does the mentorship work?+
Premium plan students can book 1:1 video sessions with industry mentors from top companies. Sessions are typically 45–60 minutes and can be used for code reviews, project feedback, career guidance, or interview preparation. You can book up to 4 sessions per month depending on your plan.
What is the refund policy?+
We offer a full 7-day money-back guarantee with no questions asked. If you complete more than 30% of the course within 7 days and still feel it isn't right for you, contact our support team and we'll process your refund within 48 hours.
Can I access courses offline?+
Yes, our mobile app (available on Android and iOS) allows you to download video lectures for offline viewing. Downloaded content stays available for 30 days without an internet connection, perfect for commuting or areas with poor connectivity.
Are there EMI options available?+
Yes! We offer no-cost EMI options through major banks and payment providers like Razorpay and Paytm. You can split your payment into 3, 6, or 12 months with zero interest. We also offer scholarships for meritorious students — apply through our scholarship form on the website.
How long do I have access to the course?+
Lifetime access! Once you enroll, you own the course forever — including all future updates, new modules, bonus content, and resources we add over time. Many of our students revisit courses months later when applying for senior roles or brushing up on skills.
